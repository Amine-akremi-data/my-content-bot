Privacy Policy — My Content Bot

Last updated: June 7, 2026

1. Introduction

My Content Bot ("the App") is an automated content publishing tool that uses the TikTok API to upload and publish AI-generated videos to TikTok accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

2. Information We Access

The App accesses only the following TikTok account information strictly necessary to operate:

  • Basic profile information (username, display name) to identify the account
  • Permission to upload and publish videos on your behalf

We do NOT access, store, or process:

  • Your password
  • Your followers or following list
  • Your private messages
  • Any personal data beyond what is listed above

3. How We Use Information

The information accessed is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Upload AI-generated video content
  • Publish posts on your behalf on a scheduled basis

4. Data Storage

The App does not store any TikTok user data on external servers. All operations are performed locally via an automated workflow (n8n) running on a private server. TikTok access tokens are encrypted at rest before being stored.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, share, or transfer your information to any third parties. The App operates exclusively between your private server and the official TikTok API.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • TikTok API (official) — for video publishing
  • Google Vertex AI (Veo) — for AI video generation
  • Google Gemini API — for content idea and script generation
  • Google Sheets API — for content management and tracking

Each service operates under its own privacy policy.

7. Data Retention

No personal data is retained by the App beyond what is necessary for operation. Access tokens are stored locally on your private server, encrypted at rest, and are never shared.

8. Your Rights

You may revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time via:

TikTok → Settings → Security → Apps and Sessions → Revoke access

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
